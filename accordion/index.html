What does FAQ mean in English?
Frequently Asked Questions - used to refer to a list of answers to typical questions that users of a Web site might ask. More from Merriam-Webster on FAQ.
Which is correct FAQ or FAQs?
FAQ is most commonly pronounced as an initialism, "F-A-Q", but may also be pronounced as an acronym, "FAQ". Web designers often label a single list of questions as an "FAQ", such as on Google Search, while using "FAQs" to denote multiple lists of questions such as on United States Treasury sites.
